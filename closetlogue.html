ClosetLogue

Say hi to your digital outfit manager.

UX Design Project
Designed as a mobile application
Created collaboratively in a group of 5

Project Overview

The ClosetLogue UX design project spanned over a period of 3 months where my group and I went through the design life cycle. It was designed with the intention of alleviating the difficulties that youths face in making their outfit decisions.

We utilised Balsamiq for wireframing and Figma for our hi-fidelity prototype. The main features included in the prototype are:

Digital Closet Feature

View your closet on the go via the Digital Closet Feature.

Get Outfit Recommendations

Get recommendations on how to dress depending on the occasion.

Engage the Community

Share your outfit with others, and let others vote on your outfits if you cannot decide.

Get Purchase Recommendations

Get recommendations on how to expand your closet, given a limited budget.
