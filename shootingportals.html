Game Overview

Pulse invented the Portal Gate, which allows one to travel to another galaxy. However, his dog, Orbit, accidentally entered the Portal Gate. It is up to the player to help Pulse bring Orbit back safely.

Play as Pulse, who has a unique kit to be explored.

10 unique levels across 4 different galaxies

Immersive storyline and cutscenes

Login and register

Customisable controls

Development

Shooting Portals was developed over a period of 3 months. The game was developed by us, but we do not own the assets where we used online assets that were either paid or free.

During the development process, we utilised the Agile Software Development Methodology where we iteratively built our game through a total of 6 sprints over 3 months.

As it was an independent learning module, we picked up our skills through online tutorials and guides. The main tech stack that was used for the game is as follows.

In the first 2.5 months, we focused mainly on developing our core features, such as the gameplay mechanics and login interface. We constantly sought feedback from our friends, improving on the features during each iteration. In the last 0.5 months, we then shifted our focus on conducting extensive testing via heuristic evaluation and usability testing.
